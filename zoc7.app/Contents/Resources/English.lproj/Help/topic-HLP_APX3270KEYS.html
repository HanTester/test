TN3270 Special Keys on the PC Keyboard

These and other TN3270 keys can also be mapped to user buttons (Options→Session Profile→User Buttons) or to any keyboard keys in the Options→Keyboard Profiles by using Emulation Dependent Key Names for Remapping They can also be sent this way from Rexx through the ZocSetEmuKey command.
